I am a junior at **Nankai University**, majoring in Software Engineering. My research interests include **Embodied Intelligence**, **Spatial Intelligence**, and **Multimodal Reasoning**. I am passionate about building AI agents that can physically interact with the world and reason through complex multimodal data.

Currently, I am a research intern at **GALBOT \| IIIS, Tsinghua University**, supervised by Prof. [**Li Yi**](https://ericyi.github.io). Previously, I was a research intern at the **Institute for AI Industry Research (AIR), Tsinghua University**, working with Prof. [**Peng Li**](https://lpeng.net) and Prof. [**Yang Liu**](https://nlp.csai.tsinghua.edu.cn/~ly/), as well as the **Pervasive Human-Computer Interaction Lab (&pi; Lab), Tsinghua University**, supervised by Prof. [**Chun Yu**](https://pi.cs.tsinghua.edu.cn/lab/people/ChunYu/).

For more details, please see my [CV](/files/dairu_cv.pdf).
